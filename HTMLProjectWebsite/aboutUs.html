About Us

At Ghurka, we view life as a never-ending series of journeys. Sometimes those journeys take us across time zones and continents; sometimes they simply take us across town. Regardless, each voyage marks us with its own indelible stamp, coloring our memories and sculpting our individual identities.

COMPANIONS FOR LIFE

We believe the journey of your life deserves worthy travel companions, such as the heirloom-quality leather bags and accessories we have been making for generations. Handcrafted by artisans using the finest materials, each piece is made not to withstand life’s adventures, but to be molded by them. As with many things in life, a Ghurka only gets better with age and experience—the beautiful patina it develops capturing sun-soaked days, rain-slicked nights and every unexpected detour in between. A lived-in and well-loved Ghurka becomes like a second passport, marked with moments large and small—what you reach for to remember past journeys, and light the spark for future endeavors.
